Draft & Drafts - Event Calendar

Weekly Events

Below you will find our finely currated offerings of play to pair with our beverage offerings.

Monday - Thursday

Casual Play - All Day
Come on down for a relaxing time of casual play and some quality draft beverages. Commander, Constructed, Trades.
Fill and Fire Draft - Starts 6pm
As soon as 8 players are signed up we will begin! Come on down and draft all night long.

Friday

Friday Night Magic (FNM) - Starts 7pm
We will be offering a trio of format offerings.
Number of rounds and prize support based on attendance.
Legacy, Modern, Standard.
Friday Night Commander - Starts 6pm
Once we have 4 players we will kick off a new pod.
Winner receives Collector Booster Pack.
Casual Play - All Day
Come on down for a relaxing time of casual play and some quality draft beverages. Commander, Constructed, Trades.

Saturday

Saturday Gauntlet - Starts 11:30am
Each Quarter we will run our Gauntlet series. First 12 weeks will be qualifiers with week 13 being the championship for that quarter.
We will begin with 4 rounds of constructed play. *** Additional rounds added as required. ***
After the rounds of constructed we will cut to Top 8.
Will then play 3 rounds to determine that weeks gauntlet champion.
(Check our Homepage on Mondays for what format will be showcased.)
Saturday Sealed - Starts 4pm
Build your deck from 6 packs of the current Standard Set.
Will be 4 rounds. Prize support based on record.
Casual Play - All Day
Come on down for a relaxing time of casual play and some quality draft beverages. Commander, Constructed, Trades.

Sunday

Sunday Lounging - All Day
We will have the TV's on with whatever program is in demand. Play, drink, relax! Whatever your choice we would love to see you.
Fill and Fire Draft - All Day
As soon as 8 players are signed up we will begin!
Sunday Highlander Showdown - 2pm
Bring your best Commander deck and compete 1v1 for a sealed box of a Standard legal set of your choice. Once you have 2 loses you are elminated. Last one standing wins the box!

Special & Upcoming Events

Check out the list of events we have planned upcoming below!

October

Halloween Horrors
Receive a variety of Innistrad plane sets for your own sealed kit equivalent (6 packs). These will be blind kits assembled beforehand from all sets based on the plane of Innistrad.
We will then cut to a Top 8 where we will draft Innistrad Remastered.
Top 2 will split prize support based on attendance.

November

Thanksgiving Leftovers
Come on down and digest while you choose your own sealed kit equivalent (6 packs) from our current inventory or current Standard Legal sets.
We will then cut to a Top 8 where we will draft something special. Don't want to ruin the surprise, but only those who master their sealed decks will make it.
Top 2 will split prize support based on attendance.

December

Twelve Drafts of Christmas
We will be digging into the vault and pulling out some old classics.
Twelve different sets, 2 boxes of each, 1 pod of 8 per box.
Prize support will consist of 12 remaining packs in each box and other promo packs and fun goodies.
