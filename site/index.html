Draft & Drafts - An adult twist on a Local Game Store.

At Draft & Drafts we offer a casual and relaxed environment for an adult crowd looking to relax and unwind playing some cards while enjoying a beverage.

We are looking to bridge the gap between getting together with your friends at the kitchen table, restaurant, tavern/bar, along with your typical local game store (LGS). We do this by offering an establishment that is 21+, nothing against those under 21, we are just not the LGS for you. At Draft & Drafts you will find a gaming environment unlike that of your typicaly LGS. With adult beverages on-tap come on in with your friends and play group to test, hangout and compete.

Draft & Drafts features:

  • Current Standard Legal Magic Sets.
  • Casual and competitive events.
  • Focus on Draft Format, though we still offer other constructed formats.
  • Quality Draft Beverages including select local craft offerings.
  • Beverages consisting of Beer, Cider, and Seltzers.
  • TV's able to display most on demand events.
  • Menus from local shops should you be hungry.
