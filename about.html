MEET THE STAFF

Arthur - Owner

Draft & Drafts came to Arthur from an idea of taking a local game store or LGS and combining it with a taproom environment. A place where you can relax with friends playing your favorite collective card game or CCG while enjoying a cold pint. While a typically a LGS is welcoming to all ages Arthur wanted to focus on providing a space with a more adult oriented.

Cassandra - Draft Manager

Cassandra comes to us with years of brewery experience. Her knowledge to crafting a delectable beverage helps to ensure we are bringing some of the best craft beers on tap and available to you, while ensuring there is plenty of variety. She is always happy to discuss new seasonal flavors.

Landon - Event Coordinator

With a passion for fun and excitement Landon works hard to coordinate events that we hope ignite your spark as much as us. If you have an idea for an event you would be excited to participate in grab Landon when you see him and share.

House Rules

  1. Respect the store and staff.
    1. Keep noise levels reasonable.
    2. Treat staff and other customers with respect, which includes using correct pronouns and avoiding discriminatory language or behavior.
    3. Do not disrupt the store's operations or damage property.
  2. Support the business.
    1. Purchase items from the store instead of bringing in your own snacks and drinks, especially if the store sells them.
    2. Avoid advertising other businesses without permission.
    3. Support the store by purchasing products or paying for play space if required.
  3. Play with integrity.
    1. Be honest and admit to misplays, even if you notice them before an opponent or judge does.
    2. Avoid cheating, manipulating game materials, or lying about the game state.
    3. Be upfront with your intentions for the game, such as if you are playing a highly competitive deck or are open to proxies.
  4. General etiquette.
    1. Clean up your play area and put all game materials back in their original places.
    2. Ask before handling someone else's cards or property.
    3. If you win, don't be obnoxious, and if you lose, don't complain excessively.
  5. Alcohol consumption.
    1. Must be 21+ with valid identification, no exceptions.
    2. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason.
    3. Please always use provided drink holders in an effort to avoid spills.
    4. All beverages poured must be consumed on the premises.
    5. Only a single pint or flight may be provided per person at a time.
